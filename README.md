# CFO Decision Intelligence Engine
### Institutional-Grade Financial Analytics System | Python | v8.3

## What This Is
A modular Python-based financial analytics engine built to replicate 
the depth of CFO-level decision making. Built independently — no 
templates, no shortcuts.

## What It Does
- Computes 35+ financial metrics across profitability, liquidity, 
  ROIC, working capital, and risk
- Runs 10,000-scenario Monte Carlo simulation to stress-test 
  EBITDA outcomes (P5, P50, P95 distributions)
- Models revenue shock scenarios — quantifies full P&L impact 
  of –20% and –40% revenue shocks
- Benchmarks 5 capital deployment options against WACC (12.8%) 
  to identify value-accretive strategy
- Builds 11 CFO decision gates across Liquidity, ROIC, DSCR, 
  and Capital Structure
- Generates board-format investment report with risk severity 
  ranking, cash sensitivity analysis, and early warning signals

## Results on Test Company
- Company: ₹29.89 Cr manufacturing firm
- Operational Health Score: 100/100
- Investment Report Score: 8.7/10
- Capital Recommendation: Core operations reinvestment 
  at ROIC 497.8% = 39× WACC
- Monte Carlo: P5 ₹9.62 Cr | P50 ₹12.20 Cr | P95 ₹14.75 Cr
